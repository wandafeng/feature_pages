AcerSense™ Key: An easy way to launch AcerSense™



What is AcerSense™ ?

AcerSense™ is a useful utility app to help you take control of and maintain your system. One click the AcerSense™ key on the keyboard and take control over your Aspire laptop.


Home

Quickly change system usage modes, check performance and battery status.



Experience Zone

A suite of AI-enhanced features for collaboration.



Personal Settings

Customize hardware settings.



Checkup

Check the current system healthy status, optimize battery usage and storage space.



App Center

Browse, add and run the installed apps.



Note:

AcerSense updated version, availability and rollout timing varies by models.

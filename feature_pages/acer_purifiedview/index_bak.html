Acer PurifiedView



Acer PurifiedView is designed to optimize your on-camera presence, delivering a smarter, clearer, and more engaging video experience—whether in professional meetings, casual conversations, or moments of personal expression.


Portrait Refinement

Bring out your best look effortlessly. Beauty 2.0 make your nose taller, reduces eye bags, blemish removal , and even makes your hair look softer—all in real time.



Glasses Reflection Removal

See your face clearly, every time. With Eyeglasses Reflection Removal, distracting reflections from screen glare are reduced, so your eyes stay visible and natural during video calls.



Avatar

Show your playful side with a cartoon-style Avatar that mirrors your expressions in real time. Whether in meetings or casual chats, it offers a fun and unique way to present yourself.



Auto Framing

When you move or adjust your position, the camera can automatically adjust the frame to ensure that your face is always in the center ofthe frame. This is very convenient for those who of ten need to move the camera or switch positions.



Eye Contact

This feature makes your gaze look more natural when you're video conferencing with your webcam. When you look at the screen, the camera automatically adjusts your line of sight so that it looks like you are looking directly at the other person.



Background Blur

This feature allows you to blur the background during a video conference, making your image more focused on yourself. This is especially useful for people who work from home or video conference with other people in a noisy environment.



Acer PurifiedView™ Settings

You can enable these features by following the steps below: Open 'Settings' > 'Bluetooth and Devices' > 'Camera' > 'Acer HD User Facing' or 'Acer FHD User Facing' or 'Acer QHD User Facing'

  1. Find the 'Auto Framing' option and enable it.
  2. Find the 'Eye Contact' option and enable it.
  3. Find the 'Background Blur' option and enable it.

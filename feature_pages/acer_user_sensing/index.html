Acer User Sensing 3.0 leverages the built-in proximity sensor integrated beside the webcam to deliver a more secure, efficient, and health-conscious user experience. It enables seamless login as you approach, automatic device lock when you step away, and intelligent reminders to support well-being during extended use.


User Sensing

Enhance both security and productivity with intelligent presence detection.

Lock on Leave

When you move away, the system automatically locks your PC, safeguarding data and conserving energy. It can also mute the microphone or power down external displays to ensure privacy.



Wake on Approach

As soon as you return, your device immediately wakes and prepares for authentication, allowing you to resume work without delay.



Adaptive Dimming

The display automatically reduces brightness when attention is not detected, extending battery life while minimizing unnecessary power consumption.



Break Reminder:

Prompts you to take regular screen breaks to reduce eye fatigue.


Seamless Multi-Screen Productivity

Cursor Teleporting

The pointer intuitively shifts to the display you are looking at when multiple monitors are in use, streamlining navigation.



Window Teleporting

Transfer application windows between displays with a glance, enabling faster and more natural multitasking.

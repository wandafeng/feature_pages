Acer Aspire Makes Everyday Computing Easy for You

It’s perfect for your daily tasks—whether that’s browsing the web, streaming videos, catching up on social media, or keeping your photos safe. With an easy-to-use design and all the essentials you need, the Aspire takes the hassle out of technology so you can focus on what matters most.




Compact and Easy to Carry

Your Aspire has a clean, compact design, so you can use it easily at your desk and slip it into your bag quickly when you need to move.



Easy-Open Design

You can easily open your Aspire using the undercut edge at the front, so you can get to work without any hassle.



Work Comfortably from Any Angle

You can flip the Aspire’s 180° hinge to any angle, making it easy to share your screen, present, or collaborate comfortably—whether you’re at your desk, in a meeting, or on the go.



More Screen, More Productivity

You get extra screen space on the slim-bezel display to help you to stay focused at work and fully enjoy movies or games afterward.



Bigger Keys, Brighter Typing

You’ll see the keys more easily and type faster with enlarged backlit keycaps in a solid color and clear, easy-to-read lettering—even in low light.



Big Touchpad, Big Convenience

With the spacious touchpad, you can scroll through documents, switch between windows, or click links with precision and ease—just like using a mouse.



Designed with Sustainability in Mind




The Aspire series is EPEAT-registered—a global ecolabel that rates electronics on energy efficiency, recyclability, and reduced hazardous materials.



It’s built with PCR plastic (Post-Consumer Recycled plastic, made from recycled materials collected from used consumer products, helping reduce waste and the need for new virgin plastic).



100% recyclable packaging, showing a strong commitment to sustainability.

Bring laptop users a more intuitive and precise way to interact: “Touch Styluses”



Why active stylus pen ?

An active stylus pen offers enhanced functionality over a finger or passive stylus, providing benefits like pressure sensitivity for varied line weights, palm rejection to prevent unintended touches, and greater precision for detailed tasks such as note-taking, illustrating, and digital art. These digital components allow for a more natural and expressive user experience, mimicking traditional pens and pencils on digital devices.


The use of active stylus pens on laptops offered by Acer provides several significant benefits

Provides precise control and input

Styluses are designed for touchscreens, with a fine tip that allows for more precise and accurate input than fingers. This makes it easier to perform delicate operations on the laptop, such as drawing, sketching, or writing with precision.



Boost productivity and creativity

A stylus is a popular tool for note-taking and drawing on laptops. When it comes to editing documents and annotating, the stylus is highly effective, providing precise control whether reviewing PDFs, marking up presentations, or editing images.



Improve ease of use and adaptability

Styluses are generally easy to use, with operations as simple as holding a pencil in hand, making it easy for even first-time users to grasp their basic functions.

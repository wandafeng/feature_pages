The slim-bezel display gives you more room to work and multitask, with vivid colors and deep blacks that make everything from documents to movies look amazing.



Typing That’s Quiet, Comfortable, and Supportive

Late-night typing won’t disturb anyone, and your video calls won’t pick up distracting key noises, thanks to the improved keycap structure and materials that reduce resonance noise. You can type comfortably and precisely on the larger, UV-coated backlit keys with optimized lettering—smooth to the touch and easy to read, even in low light.

You can work longer with effortless ease, supported by the aerospace-grade titanium frame—it’s strong, corrosion-resistant, and 30% lighter than the stainless-steel version for easier everyday use.



Smart Interactive Copilot Key Lighting

You have clear visual feedback, so you always know when AI features are active. The backlit Copilot Key blinks when pressed, and it features a breathing light effect when the NPU is processing.



Mega Touchpad, Effortless Control

The mega touchpad gives you more space for precise navigation and smooth gestures, making work and multitasking easier and more comfortable.



Multi Control with One Press

The Acer Multi-Control touchpad is a smart feature that makes your everyday tasks easier. It lights up when you watch videos or play music and automatically switches to meeting mode during online calls—helping you stay productive and look professional without any extra effort.



Designed with Sustainability in Mind

The Swift series is EPEAT-registered—a global ecolabel that rates electronics on energy efficiency, recyclability, and reduced hazardous materials.



It’s built with PCR plastic (Post-Consumer Recycled plastic, made from recycled materials collected from used consumer products, helping reduce waste and the need for new virgin plastic).



100% recyclable packaging, showing a strong commitment to sustainability.
